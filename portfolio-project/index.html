Hello, I'm Molly

Journalism and Creative Writing student


I'm Molly Williams, a journalism and creative writing student at the University of Maryland, College park. Read on to learn a little bit more about me, inside and outside the classroom.

I plan to use my education to pursue a career in audio broadcasting, specifically radio or podcasting. Through my classes and activities, I have extensively explored topics related to music through research and, most recently, an upcoming radio hour for Maryland's university station, WMUC.

I have been writing for most of my life, which is what drew me to Journalism and creative writing in college. Over the past few years, I have written a number of short stories in Maryland's writing workshops. I am well-versed in the writing and workshopping process as a result, and am able to use these skills in all the different types of writing required for my coursework and extracurricular activities.
